    Ask A Question

    Q: Is the VRLA Tech Apollo Gaming PC plug and play ready?

    A: Yes! the Apollo Gaming PC will come plug and play ready. It will have all software and drivers installed. Just make sure you have a monitor, keyboard, and mouse so you can play!



    Q: Is the VRLA Tech Apollo Gaming PC upgradeable?

    A: Absolutely, all of our PCs are upgradeable as long as the parts that you want to upgrade are compatible with the motherboard.


    Q: Is the VRLA Tech Apollo Gaming PC VR ready?

    A: Yes! 


    Q: Does the VRLA Tech Apollo Gaming PC come with WiFi?

    A: The Apollo Gaming PC does not come with WiFi. You will need to select the upgrade option at checkout to make sure your PC can connect to WiFi. If you do not purchase the WiFi upgrade option then your computer can still connect to the internet but you will be required to connect an ethernet cable to the router.


    Q: Does the VRLA Tech Apollo Gaming PC come with Bluetooth?

    A: Apollo does not come standard with Bluetooth however Bluetooth is available as an upgrade option!


    Q: If I upgrade to “2 x fans” will I get 4 fans?

    A: No, your PC will come installed with the exact amount of fans that you choose in the upgrade options. This means if you select “2 fans” you will receive 2 fans, not 2 fans in addition to the 2 fans that are included in your build. You must choose the total number of fans you want in the customization options.


    Q: What is the difference between RGB and LED fans?

    A: RGB fans change colors while LED fans stay glowing white. You can watch this video for more information.


    Q: Can I choose a different case?

    A: We are happy to make any changes to your PC that you want. Please contact us and let us know which case you would like to use and we will do our best to accommodate your request.


    Q: Can my PC have Windows 10 activated instead of Windows 11?

    A: Absolutely, please contact us so we can make the change to your order.


    Q: Does the VRLA Tech Apollo Gaming PC come as pictured?

    A: Please check all the pictures. We have pictures that show the PC with upgrade options, standard, as well as different case colors. Please click the image and read the caption to see what your PC will look like.


    Q: Does the VRLA Tech Apollo Gaming PC have a warranty?

    A: Yes! All PCs have a 1 year warranty. Please click here for more information


    Q: Can I ship to an address that is not my billing address?

    A: No, we do not ship to an address that is not on file with your credit card provider. If you would like to place an order and ship to a different address you can contact your credit card provider and add an additional address to your account or you can place your order with shipping to the billing address and contact us to ship your order to your nearest FedEx pickup location.


    Q: What company do you ship with? Will I be required to sign for delivery?

    A: All orders are shipped with FedEx Ground or FedEx Express depending on the shipping method that you choose at checkout. You will be required to sign for delivery so please track your order to make sure you are home for delivery.


    Q: Am I able to pick up my order instead of having it shipped?

    A: Yes. Please place your order and contact us so we can set up an appointment for pickup. If you are charged for shipping upon checkout you will be refunded your shipping fee once your order is picked up.


    Q: If I change the components in my PC after I receive it will it void my warranty?

    A: Yes, the warranty that is attached to your PC is only applicable to the components that your PC comes with. If you make changes to the components you will void your warranty.


    Q: Can I overclock my PC?

    A: Yes, you may overclock your PC but please note that overclocking will void your warranty.


    Q: I am located outside of the USA, will the power cable work anywhere?

    A: No, the power cable is compatible with USA power outlets. You will need to purchase an adapter that will fit into your required power outlet.

    Meet Apollo

    A powerful prebuilt gaming PC which features top of the line gaming performance at less than top of the line price. The Apollo allows you to experience high end gaming without breaking the bank. This custom Gaming PC comes equipped with components from well-known manufacturers in PC gaming. Upgrade your NVMe SSD and GPU to an Nvidia GeForce RTX 3060 Ti for an even better Apollo!


    Harness the Power

    Get next-gen parts, for last gen prices. The Apollo gets you 1440p gaming for an incredible price. One of our best price-to-performance values, the Apollo gets you everything you need and nothing you don’t. Featuring the Ryzen 5 5600X, a 6 core 12 thread processor that utilizes AMD’s lauded Zen 3 architecture. With a base clock of 3.7GHz, and boost of 4.6GHz, this ultra fast processor is just as good at single threaded tasks as it is multi-threaded.

    We seat this excellent processor into a B450 motherboard, which is still the best choice for AM4 socket builds. It has all the features you need for a modern system, without extra unnecessary features that would make this great value more expensive. By default, we slot in 16 GB of DDR4 RAM, which is just what you need for modern systems. If you’re a content creator or do a lot of RAM heavy tasks, we recommend upgrading to at least 32GB.


    Multitask Game, Stream, Work

    The Apollo comes standard with a GeForce RTX 3060, the best mid-range GPU on the market. Play games on high settings in 1440p with nearly 100 FPS. Want extreme frame rates? Play in 1080p and watch them shoot up into the 300’s. Whether you’re looking for realistic graphics or a competitive edge, the Apollo can do it all. Plus, when you purchase an Apollo you get NVIDIA’s latest generation of ray tracing. With DLSS 2.0, you can experience ray traced graphics without the performance hit.


    Cool Case Design

    The Apollo is an affordable PC that welcomes everyone into the PC Gaming world, even for gamers who want to tinker with their system. Our High Performance Apollo ARGB Tower CPU Cooler is a powerful air cooler that sits on top of your CPU, allowing you to enjoy the full power of your Ryzen CPU and maintain cool temps while doing it, overclocked or not. That, in conjunction with the cooling performance of our Deepcool Matrexx 55 RGB case, you’ll find that you’re able to get that extra bit of speed out of your Apollo whenever you need it should you choose to overclock or not.

    The case is a perfect blend of a minimalist home PC and a gaming PC with a tempered glass side panel to show of your PC. It also has optimized airflow with large air intakes on either side of the front panel with two exhausts, both at the back and the top to keep everything cool on the inside.
